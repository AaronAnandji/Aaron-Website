About Me

I'm a Canadian startup and tech enthusiast known as a super-connector and community builder, driven by a passion for meaningful execution. My journey through the innovation ecosystem has spanned a range of roles and initiatives.

Currently, I serve as a semi-regular contributor to BetaKit, Canada's leading innovation publication, and as a partner at Dorm Room Fund, where I help identify and support promising student entrepreneurs.

Previously, I was employee No. 21 at CheckSammy, where I built the company's waste audit program and developed customized sustainability solutions for enterprise clients. I also founded McGill Ventures, McGill University's premier innovation community, dedicated to fostering the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Looking ahead, I'm focused on deepening my expertise in investing and operations, while continuing to build bridges across Canada's tech ecosystem.
